Who we are

Safety Port is a leading Saudi company dedicated to providing comprehensive solutions in Personal Protection Equipment and road safety equipment. Established with a commitment to excellence, Safety Port has emerged as a trusted partner in ensuring the safety and security of individuals and communities across Saudi Arabia.

Our Mission
We aspire to be the premier provider of safety solutions in Saudi Arabia, recognized for our unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction, technological advancement, and societal well-being.
Our Vision
At Safety Port, our mission is to safeguard lives and enhance safety standards by delivering high-quality and innovative products, tailored to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

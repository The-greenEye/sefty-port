Personal Protection Equipment

Safety Glasses /Goggles

Shield the eyes from debris, chemicals, or harmful light.

Safety Helmets

Protect head from impacts and falling objects.

Respirators/Masks

Filter out harmful particles, gases, or vapors from the air.

Earplugs / Ear Muffs

Reduce exposure to loud noises, protecting hearing.

Gloves

Protect hands from cuts, abrasions, chemicals, or temperature extremes

Face Shields

Protect the face from splashes, flying debris, or chemical splashes.

Safety Footwear

Reinforced shoes to protect against impact, punctures, or electrical hazards

Visibility / Reflective vest

Clothing with reflective strips for visibility in low-light conditions.

Protective Clothing/Coveralls

Full-body suits to protect against hazardous materials or contaminants.

First Aid Kit

Supplies used to provide medical treatment in emergency situations.

Harnesses and Fall Arrest

Prevent falls from heights in construction or climbing.

Fire Extinguishers

Fire extinguishers are essential safety devices designed to suppress fires in emergency situations

Road / Traffic Safety Equipment

Safety Barriers

Barricades, guardrails, and crash barriers to protect workers and motorists from hazards.

Traffic Cones / Bollards

Used to delineate traffic lanes, redirect traffic, or mark hazards.

Road Signs

Regulatory signs, warning signs, used to convey rules, hazards, and directions to road users

Reflective Tape and Markings

Applied to vehicles, signs, and barriers to improve visibility in low-light conditions

Traffic Lights

Placed at road intersections or pedestrian crossings to manage the traffic flow

Speed Bumps/Speed Humps

Installed on roads to reduce vehicle speed in certain areas, such as school zones or residential neighborhoods.

Traffic Mirrors

Placed at intersections or blind spots to improve visibility for drivers and pedestrians

Construction Netting

Installed around the perimeter of construction sites or at higher levels of buildings under construction to prevent objects from falling

Indicative Plates

Provides information or guidance. Indicative Plates plays a crucial role in enhancing safety and improving navigation.

Cat Eyes / Road Studs

Embedded in the road surface to improve visibility and delineate lanes, especially during low light conditions or poor visibility to enhance safety on roads.

Flashlights

enhances safety, visibility, traffic control, emergency signaling, in work sites.
